Send me a color Mechanical gestures to preserve flowers:
An essay on color reproduction using contact duplication systems

Film performance · research project
16mm film · 12 min · 2024
Director Mariana Torres
Production Elías Querejeta Zine Eskola
Synopsis

It is a filmic performance that explores the mechanical gestures of 16mm projectors to reveal light as a material origin and color as an empirical exploration of vision.
