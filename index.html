MARIANA TORRES

Audiovisual Archivist & Moving Image Practitioner

I work between film archives, analogue technologies and moving image practice. My interests cross preservation, projection, photochemical experimentation and the afterlives of militant and countercultural cinema.

This portfolio gathers artistic works, research, and analogue film practice, alongside documentation of projects developed with archives, festivals and cultural institutions.
