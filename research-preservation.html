RESEARCH & PRESERVATION WORKS

Archival study · reconstruction · cataloguing · historical research

Archival Investigations

Research projects carried out within film archives, involving identification, inspection, cataloguing and contextual analysis of analogue film materials.

  • Filmographic reconstruction based on incomplete or fragmentary reels.
  • Inspection reports: shrinkage, perforation damage, base type, sound formats.
  • Contextual research on militant, countercultural and orphan film collections.

Preservation & Restoration Processes

Workflows documenting the steps involved in stabilising, duplicating and preparing film elements for digitisation or photochemical preservation.

  • Cleaning and repair of film elements prior to scanning or duplication.
  • Registry of printer-lights, shrinkage measurements and splice types.
  • Collaborative preservation projects with institutions and film labs.

Case Studies & Research Notes

Notes from ongoing investigations into Portuguese and international archives, including unfinished films, activist cinema and alternative film cultures.

  • Reconstruction notes for incomplete or abandoned film projects.
  • Analysis of archival metadata and provenance documentation.
  • Comparative study between analogue and digital reconstruction strategies.
