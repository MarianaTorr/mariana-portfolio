About

I work with moving image preservation, analogue film practices, and archival processes, focusing on the transmission of photochemical knowledge and its contemporary relevance. My practice intersects archival research, expanded cinema, and contemporary digital environments.

Film preservation specialist with a Master’s degree from Elías Querejeta Zine Eskola (2023), supported by the AMIA George Blood LP Women in Audiovisual Archiving and Technology Scholarship. And a bachelor in Film Studies at ESTC (2021).

Experience in analog, digital film restoration, and archival research for the Portuguese Cinematheque, through the restoration company Cineric P. Participated in various audiovisual projects as editing assistant. Short-term research grant from the Portuguese Cinematheque.
